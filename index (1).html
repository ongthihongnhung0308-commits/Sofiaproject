What is this?

Paste any prompt you've written for an AI tool. The Prompt Debugger will rewrite it using best practices and then give you structured feedback grounded in ZPD and Feedback Theory from Second Language Acquisition research.

Designed for language teachers, teacher trainers, and educators integrating AI into their classrooms.

💡 Before you start

  • Write your prompt as you normally would — don't pre-polish it.
  • It can be for any AI tool: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.
  • Be specific about your learning goals, audience, and level.
  • The more context you give, the richer the feedback will be.
